Our History

Avery Excavating, LLC owned and operated by Kyle Avery Riegle has well over 15 years of leading excavation experience from small residential projects, to multi 10 million dollar underground utilities renovations. Working as an equipment operator and as a supervisor and job foreman within many projects over the last decade, Avery Excavating has the experience you demand. The highest quality standards and customer service are “Bar-Non” the best in the industry.


Kyle started his excavating career at the young age of 2 years old…

“I always loved Tonka toys! My dad built me a sand box when I was about 2 years old and it was always my favorite place to play with all of my tonka trucks. When we moved to Michigan when I was 4 years old I had a “full range” mini sand pit behind our garage. I was about 10 years old when I decided on a hot summer day, to build a swimming pool. I dug the entire sand pit out with “hot tub precision seating” I layed the dirt hole with a tarp and filled it with water. By 4pm in the heat of the afternoon I had a fully functioning swimming pool behind the garage”

Summers after I worked on the family Cherry farm hauling cherries and operating cherry harvesting equipment. After high School I began working at AIS Construction equipment in Rental Sales. About a year and a half later I made the move to Florida where I worked for an underground Utility service and installation company contracted by Florida Power to work on installing underground power throughout the Tampa Bay area.
